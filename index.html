Rabbit

A rabbit’s teeth never stop growing! Many people believe they need to chew to keep their teeth short. While they do enjoy chewing, it’s the normal wear from where their top and bottom teeth meet that keeps a rabbit’s teeth short.

  • Scientific Family Name: Liporidae
  • Average Sleep Time: 8.4 hours per day
  • Average Lifespan: 8 years
  • Habitat: Meadows, woods, forests, grasslands, deserts and wetlands.

A baby rabbit is called a kit, a female is called a doe, and a male is a buck. A group of rabbits is called a herd. Rabbits are herbivores, eating a diet entirely of grasses and other plants. Bunnies cannot vomit, so it is super important to feed them only healthy, fresh, appropriate food.
